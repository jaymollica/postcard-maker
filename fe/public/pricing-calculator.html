Lob pricing includes print + postage (USPS rates as of July 2025). All sizes shown use First Class postage. Lob does not currently offer a 5x7 size; 6x9 and 6x11 are larger alternatives.

International shipping is only available for 4x6 postcards through Lob. International orders also automatically include a US return address (required by Lob).

Stripe fee is calculated as 2.9% + $0.30 for US-issued cards. Toggle "foreign-issued card" to add Stripe's 1.5% international card fee. International postcards default to assuming a foreign card.
